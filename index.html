Ninjaijo Dev – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2025

Thank you for playing our games. This Privacy Policy explains how Ninjaijo Dev ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile games.

Information We Collect

Our games may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID).
  • Usage Data: Gameplay activity, interactions, and general app usage statistics.
  • Advertising Data: Data collected by advertising partners to display ads.
  • Photos & Media: Images saved by the user are stored locally on the device and are not collected or transmitted by us.

Advertising Services

Our games display advertisements using the following services:

Google AdMob

Google AdMob may collect information such as device identifiers and IP address to provide personalized or non-personalized advertisements.

Unity Ads

Unity Ads may collect and process information in accordance with their privacy policy to deliver advertising services.

Learn more about how our advertising partners handle data:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Unity Privacy Policy: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain our games
  • To improve gameplay and user experience
  • To display advertisements
  • To analyze usage and performance
  • To ensure security and prevent abuse

Data Sharing

We may share information with:

  • Advertising partners (Google AdMob and Unity Ads)
  • Analytics and service providers
  • Authorities when required by law

Children’s Privacy

Our games are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such data is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
